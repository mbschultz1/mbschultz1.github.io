How it all works | 怎麼運行的

Online courses at a virtual academy: Mandarin Playground offers online sessions between 4pm-8pm PST from Monday to Sunday all year round. Programs range from PreK-12, ages 3-15. Each program has two sessions per week, 30-min sessions for young students, and 60-min sessions for older students.
